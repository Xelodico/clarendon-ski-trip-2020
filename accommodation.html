Convenient location

  • Tourist hotel
  • 🌟🌟🌟
  • Free Wifi
  • Located in the city centre
  • Pool, gym and sauna available
  • Breakfast available
We have chosen this hotel for our accomodation since it's the most suitable for the activities we're doing.
Being located in the city centre, it's conveniently only a 4-minute walk from Le Brévent ski area lifts and an 8-minute walk from Montenvers Railway, which offers service to the Mer de Glace glacier (more info can be found in the itinerary page).
An airport shuttle is available, therefore no booking for private coaches is required.

Suitable rooms





The hotel has the perfect rooms for our ski trip. The wood adds a homely feel which makes you think you're staying in your own cabin!
Some rooms also feature a balcony with a view of the mountains.
A TV, wi-fi, and telephone are included in all rooms.









Why go here?








Wide range of amentities




The hotel includes a wide range of amentities such as sauna, a gym, a pool table and especially ski storage.
There is also a games room that includes a climbing wall, pool table and more!
